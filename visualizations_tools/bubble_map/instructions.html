AdvisR

Welcome to AdvisR, an interactive visualization of multiple FlickR datasets.

.

1. On the main map, the geographical distribution of photo's is shown.

2. Click on a country to zoom in.

3. Hover over a bubble to see the selected photo.

\

4. At the bottom right, click on the box to see the most popular photo's in that country.

5. Click on the previously selected country to zoom out to the world view again.

6. Choose a label from the sunburst diagram to see only photo's with that label.

7. Use the slider to see only photo's that were taken in the selected months.

8. Click on a specific image bubble to see similar images (sharing tags, groups, galleries, or collections).
